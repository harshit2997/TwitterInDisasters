Second Quake Hits Nepal

Nepal has been hit with a second earth quake within a month.

Witnesses say many people have been injured and several killed.

Rose Foley from Unicef in Kathmandu has described another day of terror for the country:

Today's tremor comes just two weeks after more than 8,000 people were killed in a massive quake that struck the region.

The latest quake happened at lunch, local time and measured 7.3 in magnitude.

It hit near Mount Everest, at a shallow depth of about 15 kilometers.

According to Nepal's information minister, at least 16 people have been killed and 846 injured, with the tremor felt in northern India and Bangladesh.
