<headline>
Prime Minister calls for volunteers
</headline>

<text>
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremasinghe has requested to inform if there are any doctors,

nurses or assistants who are willing to provide relief for the earth quake affected people in Nepal. He has issued a special statement making this request. Prime Minister Ranil Wickremasinghe this morning spoke to Nepalese President Ram Barron Ydhow and expressed his sorrow for the people in Nepal and pledged to provide maximum assistance. The Prime Minister has requested doctors, nurses and assistants who are willing to volunteer their services for the people in Nepal, to inform over the telephone number 0712 492 484. The Prime Minister has requested Sri Lankan medical students in Nepal to assist the people in the maximum way they can. Further details can be obtained from the Sri Lankan Embassy in Nepal.
</text>
