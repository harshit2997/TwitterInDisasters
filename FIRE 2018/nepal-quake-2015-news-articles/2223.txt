<headline>
How you can donate to Nepal earthquake relief effort
</headline>

<text>
More than 2,500 people have died following the 7.8 magnitude earthquake that struck Nepal on Saturday.

It's the worst earthquake ever to hit the Himalayan country, which now needs aid urgently.

Find out how you can donate to the Nepal earthquake relief effort here:

World Vision

Donations to World Vision's Nepal Earthquake appeal can be made by clicking at www.worldvision.org.nz

UNICEF

UNICEF is mobilizing an urgent response to meet the needs of children affected by Saturday's 7.9 earthquake in Kathmandu, Nepal. Visit www.unicef.org.nz/Nepal to donate.

Oxfam

Oxfam is accepting donations for a disaster response emergency fund online at www.oxfam.org.nz or by phoning 0800 600 700.

Tear Fund

Tear Fund is also accepting donations online at http://www.tearfund.org.nz/

Red Cross
