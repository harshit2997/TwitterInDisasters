<headline>
Pittsfield family relieved after couple hiking in Nepal survives quake
</headline>

<text>
A Pittsfield family is breathing a sigh of relief this morning after their loved ones made contact following a harrowing 48 hours in the wake of Saturday's devastating earthquake in Nepal.

"Great news!" said grateful dad Terry Holland in an email to the Herald. "They’re out! They’re safe!"
