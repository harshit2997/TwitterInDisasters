Azerbaijan evacuates its citizens from NepalThe government of Azerbaijan and the country's embassy in India are doing everything necessary to evacuate fellow citizens from Nepal, which was hit by earthquake on April 25. The death toll has reached 3218 people.Azerbaijani president Ilham Aliyev instructed the Ministry of Emergency Situations to carry out the evacuation of citizens from the earthquake zone, the head of the press service of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Azerbaijan, Hikmet Hajiyev, said.According to preliminary information, 14 citizens of Azerbaijan, including 6 children, live in Nepal, one of the children was slightly injured, but now his condition is good, Trend reports.

The government of Azerbaijan and the country's embassy in India are doing everything necessary to evacuate fellow citizens from Nepal, which was hit by an earthquake on April 25. The death toll has reached 3218 people.



Azerbaijani President Ilham Aliyev instructed the Ministry of Emergency Situations to carry out an evacuation of Azerbaijani citizens from the earthquake zone, the head of the press service of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Azerbaijan, Hikmet Hajiyev, said.



According to preliminary information, 14 citizens of Azerbaijan, including 6 children, live in Nepal, one of the children was slightly injured, but now his condition is good, Trend reports.
