<headline>
Dainik Jagran sets up 'Jagran Rahat Kosh' for Nepal earthquake victims
</headline>

<text>
For this, Jagran Prakashan Ltd has contributed Rs one crore to the relief operations being carried out in the tragedy-stricken Nepal.

Also, it has appealed Indian citizens to come forward and donate to the 'Jagran Rahat Kosh'.



Cash donations, however, will not be accepted. People are requested to deposit their funds through either cheque or draft in favour of 'Jagran Rahat Kosh' at the regional office at D-210-211, Sector 63, Noida, Uttar Pradesh, Pin: 201301.



The deposited funds will be sent to the Prime Minister's Relief Fund. Also, the fund is exempted from all the taxes under Income Tax Act-80 (G).
