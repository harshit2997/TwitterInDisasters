<headline>
Chinese leaders extends condolences to Nepal
</headline>

<text>
CCTV.com

04-26-2015 10:28 BJT

Chinese President Xi Jinping and Premier Li Keqiang have sent condolences to Nepalese President Ram Baran Yadav and Prime Minister Sushil Koirala on the major loss of life.

President Xi also said that under the leadership of President Yadav's government, the Nepalese people will jointly weather the difficulties and conquer the natural disaster.

In Premier Li's message, he expressed his deep sympathy to the relatives of the quake victims and the injured. He said China is ready to help Nepal cope with the aftermath.
</text>
