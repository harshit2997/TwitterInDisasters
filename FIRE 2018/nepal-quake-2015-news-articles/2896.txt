<headline>
Hopes for Wagga woman missing in Nepal
</headline>

<text>
Unconfirmed reports suggest missing Wagga woman Athena Zelandonii has survived the devastating earthquake in Nepal and is safe in an Langtang hospital. Picture: Facebook

REPORTS have emerged that a Wagga woman missing in devastated Nepal is safe, sheltering in a hospital in the Langtang area.



Athena Zelandonii remains listed on the Red Cross missing persons website, however unofficial reports through social media suggest she is on a list of 84 survivors.



Ms Zelandonii, a photojournalist, recently graduated from university and is travelling through South East Asia on an open ended tour.



The catastrophic 7.8 magnitude earthquake, with significant aftershocks, has claimed 3500 lives to date.
