<headline>
Relief materials to reach quake victims soon: Nepal PM
</headline>

<text>
CCTV.com

05-12-2015 17:34 BJT

Nepal's government has announced a relief package for victims of last month's earthquake. Prime Minister Sushil Koirala has promised that relief materials would 2-be distributed to quake victims in an effective manner and at the earliest possible date.

The prime minister said at a meeting of the Poor Household Support Coordination Board that the government is committed to distributing relief materials to the real victims, by identifying their status.

According to the executive vice-chairman of the board, they will immediately begin household surveys to find the real quake-affected districts.

Nepalese media reports say people in these districts will be classified into three different categories and relief materials will be provided to them.
