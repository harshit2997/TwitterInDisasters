<headline>
KPOP NEWS - Shinhwa′s Kim Dong Wan Donates to Help Victims of Nepal Earthquake
</headline>

<text>
Shinhwa′s Kim Dong Wan was revealed to have donated to help the people in Nepal.

On April 30, Korea Food for the Hungry International stated, "Kim Dong Wan contacted us saying he couldn′t sit still hearing about the tragic news in Nepal. As KFHI knows the situation in Nepal the best, he sent in a donation on April 29, asking for it to used where it was needed the most."



Kim Dong wan donated 36 million won for Nepal.



KFHI also added that Kim Dong Wan has been continuously donating to the NGO since 2007.



The donation will be used to help the victims of the Nepal earthquake by providing food and necessities and aiding in the restoration process.
