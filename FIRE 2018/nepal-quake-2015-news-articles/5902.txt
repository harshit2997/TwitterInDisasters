<headline>
ICCO provides relief aid in Nepal
</headline>

<text>
ICCO/Kerk in Actie is providing for relief aid in the Kathmandu Valley after the massive earthquake. Tents, food and blankets are distributed through ACT Alliance.

ACT is an international partnership of aid agencies, which are collectively working on relief and reconstruction after this disaster.

Saturday April 25, Nepal was hit by a massive earthquake. According to the latest UN figures 70,000 houses are destroyed and half a million houses badly damaged. As a result almost 2.8 million Nepalese people are homeless.
</text>
