<headline>
Pakistan establishes fund for Nepal quake victims
</headline>

<text>
Islamabad: The government has established a fund namely "Prime Minister's Relief Fund for Earthquake Affected People of Nepal-2015".

All proceeds on account of a fund will be received at all branches of State Bank of Pakistan, all Treasuries and all branches of National Bank of Pakistan (NBP) and all branches of all other scheduled banks, says an office memorandum issued by Cabinet Division.

All proceeds received in the name of the Fund will be credited to the public account of the Federal Government under the following head of account: G 12153 Prime Minister's Relief Fund for Earthquake Affected People of Nepal-2015.
</text>
