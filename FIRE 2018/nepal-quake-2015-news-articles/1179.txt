Art of Living helps quake victims

Bengaluru, April 27, 2015, DHNS 0:29 IST

The Art of Living centre in Kathmandu has been converted into a shelter camp following the earthquake. Over 200 people, who have lost their homes, stayed there on Saturday night.



The organisation is providing food, shelter and other relief material. The volunteers of Art of Living-Nepal are on blood donation drives at Dir Hospital in Kathmandu, which is the largest hospital, according a press release from the organisation in Bengaluru.



People can contribute and reach out to the affected in Nepal through www.iahv.org/us-en/nepal-earthquake-relief, the release said.
