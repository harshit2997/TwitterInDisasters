<headline>
Serzh Sargsyan sent telegram of condolences to President of Nepal | ARMENPRESS Armenian News Agency
</headline>

<text>
YEREVAN, 27 APRIL, ARMENPRESS. Today on 27 April, the President of the Republic of Armenia Serzh Sargsyan has sent a telegram of condolence to the President of the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal Ram Baran Yadavi on the human losses and destruction due to the devastating earthquake in the country. The Department of Public Relations and Mass Media of the Office of the President of the Republic of Armenia reports to “Armenpress”, that the President's telegram of condolence particularly runs as follows;

"Sharing your sorrow and the friendly people of Nepal, I wish endurance and courage of spirit to the families of the victims and speedy recovery to the wounded."
</text>
