<headline>
Secretary General of the International Federation of RCRC Pays 3 day-visit to Nepal
</headline>

<text>
The International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Secretary General Mr. Elhadj As Sy arrived in Kathmandu today for three-day visit of Nepal.

Sanjeev Thapa, Chairman of the Nepal Red Cross Society (NRCS), warmly welcomed to the Secretary General of the Federation Mr. Sy and his team at the Central Office of NRCS. He was accompanied by Mr. Jagan Chapagain, the Head of Asia Pacific Regional Delegation and Simon Missiri, the Head of South Asia regional Delegation.

With his welcome remarks, Chairman Thapa highlighted the support of the International Federation of the Red Cross and a great contribution from the Secretary-General Mr. Sy for the earthquake response operation of Nepal Red Cross Society. Mr. Sy observed the Emergency Operation Center of Nepal Red Cross which was established right after the quake of 25th April and received the information from various sectors like shelter, Food, assessment, communications, Restoring Family Links, Volunteer Mobilization and Health and WASH.

Today SG Sy also pay courtesy calls to the Rt. Honorable Prime Minister Sushil Koirala and Honorable Foreign Minister Mahendra Bahadur Pandey.

Earlier, Secretary General of NRCS Mr. Dev Ratna Dhakhwa had welcomed to Mr. Sy and his team at the airport.

He is returning back to Geneva on 17th May 2015.
