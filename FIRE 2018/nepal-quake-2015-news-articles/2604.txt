Economical to donate $10,000 to relief efforts in Nepal

Waterloo, Ontario -- April 28, 2015

Economical Insurance has announced the donation of $10,000 to the Canadian Red Cross to support relief efforts in the wake of the recent earthquake in Nepal.

"Our thoughts are with those who have been affected by this tragedy," says Karen Gavan, Economical's president and CEO. "Today, we join Canadians all across the country in sending our support to the people of Nepal at this difficult time."

For more information on Economical Insurance, please visit economical.com.
