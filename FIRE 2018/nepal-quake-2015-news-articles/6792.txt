<headline>
Rs.4.53 crore disbursed at CM’s Karuthal
</headline>

<text>
more-in

Chief Minister Oommen Chandy disbursed Rs.4.53 crore as assistance to aggrieved people during his mass contact programme in the district.

The programme Karuthal 2015 had received 20,057 applications from aggrieved people.

The programme, held at the sprawling municipal stadium grounds here, stretched up to 11 p.m. on Thursday.

Visits Irshad’s house

The Chief Minister after an overnight stay here visited the bereaved family of A.S. Irshad, the young physician who lost his life in the recent Kathmandu earthquake, at their residence at Anabagilu here.

Mr. Chandy also made time to visit the Taj exhibition under way here, before proceeding to Kannur.
