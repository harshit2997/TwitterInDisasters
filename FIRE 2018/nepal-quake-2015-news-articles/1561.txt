The devastating, magnitude-7.8 earthquake that hit Nepal on Saturday has left at least 4,000 dead with many more feared to have perished. People around the world have watched with horror as aid workers continue to search for casualties, assess the damage and bring relief to those injured and made homeless by the quake.

We urge those who can to support the many relief organizations who are working in Nepal. Below, we offer these thoughts, prayers and meditations in support of the suffering people of Nepal from religious leaders, activists, and many others.
