<headline>
Organ donation saves many lives: official
</headline>

<text>
more-in

Srikakulam Additional Joint Collector P. Rajanikanta Rao on Tuesday said organ and blood donation would provide satisfaction to donors and help in saving lives of many patients. He was participating as chief guest at the awareness camp on organ donation held in Nagarjuna Agrichem Private Limited, Arinam Akkivalasa village, in Srikakulam district.

Speaking on the occasion, he congratulated NACL vice-president C. Varadarajulu for conducting many blood donation camps with the support of employees. He presented silver medal and appreciation letters on behalf of the Indian Red Cross Society.

NACL deputy general manager K. Koteswara Rao, NACL PRO V.V.S.S. Prasad also received appreciation letters from Mr. Rajanikanta Rao for organising the blood donation camps. Mr. Varadarajulu said organ and blood donation would certainly help in saving seriously injured persons in disasters like earthquake in Nepal.

He handed over Rs.1 lakh cheque to Indian Red Cross Society chairman P. Jaganmohana Rao as part of CSR activity of the plant.
