<headline>
Family of missing trekker to head to Nepal
</headline>

<text>
THE family of an Australian trekker still missing after the Nepalese earthquake will travel to the Himalayas to try to find him.

TYRONNE White, 44, from Taree on the NSW mid north coast, was last seen alive in a remote village on the morning the 7.8-magnitude quake struck on April 25.
