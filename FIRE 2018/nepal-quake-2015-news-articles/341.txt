<headline>
Death toll nears 1,200 in Nepal's massive earthquake
</headline>

<text>


***UPDATE***





Nepal police say 1,394 confirmed dead in massive earthquake.





***END UPDATE***





Officials expect the number of people killed in Nepal's

massive earthquake to rise as reports come in from far-flung areas.





More than 1,100 are known dead in Nepal and at least 50 more were killed in neighboring countries. Hundreds more filled Nepal's hospitals after the magnitude 7.8 quake toppled ancient temples and modern houses and triggered an avalanche that socked a Mount Everest base camp.



The magnitude 7.8 earthquake originated outside Nepal

's capital, Kathmandu, and rattled northern parts of India, Bangladesh, Tibet and Pakistan.
