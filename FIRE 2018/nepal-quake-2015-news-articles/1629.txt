<headline>
Poroshenko dispatches plane to Nepal to bring Ukrainians home
</headline>

<text>
Ukrainian President Petro Poroshenko has instructed the leadership of the Foreign Ministry and the Defense Ministry to dispatch a plane to Nepal to evacuate Ukrainian citizens.

"I instructed the foreign minister and defense minister to send a plane to Nepal to pick up Ukrainians affected [by the earthquake] and take them home," Poroshenko wrote on Facebook on Sunday evening.

He added that Ukraine grieves with the families of all the victims of the earthquake.
</text>
