<headline>
Rogers looks to help in wake of growing disaster in Nepal
</headline>

<text>
Rogers is waiving the cost of customer calls to Nepal.

Rogers is looking to help in the wake of the growing humanitarian disaster in earthquake-stricken Nepal.

Canadians can donate to the Canadian Red Cross by texting REDCROSS to 45678 for a $10 or 30333 for a $5 donation.

To make a donation to UNICEF, text NEPAL to 45678 ($10).

Users can also make a donation to the UN World Food Programme by texting RELIEFNEPAL to 45678 ($10).

Donations will be tacked onto your monthly phone bill.

The company will also cover the cost of all calls to Nepal, to help customers in get in touch with family and friends in the region.

Rogers is the parent company of 660News.
</text>
