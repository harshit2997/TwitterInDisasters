Haiti - Diplomacy : Martelly expresses the Haiti Solidarity to the people of Nepal





The President of the Republic, Michel Martelly, has learned with dismay the news of the 7.8 magnitude earthquake that struck Nepal, Saturday, April 25, 2015, leaving more than 5,000 victims and more than 10,000 wounded, according to the latest information.



The Head of State, that deplores this tragedy, sends the deepest sympathies of the Government and People of Haiti to the Government and to the Nepalese people, especially parents and relatives of the disappeared.



The President of the Republic took the opportunity to express the Haiti solidarity to the Brotherly people of Nepal whose current suffering, remember, the hard times experienced by Haitians during the earthquake of January 12, 2010.
