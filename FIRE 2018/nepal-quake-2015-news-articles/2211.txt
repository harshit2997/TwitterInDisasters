Urgent appeal after Nepal quake horror
</headline>

<text>
A plea for funds to help survivors of the Nepal earthquake has been issued by the Disasters Emergency Committee The 7.8-magnitude earthquake has left more than 4,000 people dead and a million potentially displaced.
