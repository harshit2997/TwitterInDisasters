2nd Nepal Earthquake: Death toll rises above 125
</headline>

<text>
Nepal authorities said 110 people died in the country while 17 people were killed in India and 1 person was killed in China.

128 people have now been confirmed dead following the 7.3-magnitude earthquake that struck Nepal earlier in the week.

Nepal authorities said 110 people died in the country while 17 people were killed in India and 1 person was killed in China.

Over 2,500 people were also injured, this is according to Nepali Home Ministry spokesman Laxmi Prasad Dhakal.

The country had still been dealing with the 7.8-magnitude earthquake which struck the country 2 and half weeks earlier, killing over 8,000, before the 2nd quake struck.
