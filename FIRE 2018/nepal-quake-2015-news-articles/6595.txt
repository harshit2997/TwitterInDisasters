<headline>
Binyamin Netanyahu Speaks at Anti-Semitism Conference
</headline>

<text>
Prime Minister Binyamin Netanyahu took part in today's (Tuesday) Global Forum for Combating Antisemitism.

"We are living in the generation in which anti-Semitism returned to live," said Netanyahu. He added that Jews should be able to freely live where they choose, and that it is up to the governments of the world to ensure their safety.

The Prime Minister also used last month's earthquake in Nepal to criticize Iran, asking, "Did anyone see an Iranian delegation in Nepal? Iran is the perfect example of the attacker blaming the victim."
