<headline>
Earthquake survivor at AIIMS
</headline>

<text>
NEW DELHI: A 25-year-old medical student who survived the Kathmandu earthquake was airlifted and admitted to AIIMS Trauma Center on Wednesday.Abin Suri, who belongs to Kozhikode in Kerala, had gone to Nepal with some friends to attend a conference.Doctors said his spine and lower limbs were fractured, and kidneys crushed. "He has multiple fractures and crush injuries but he was brought here primarily because of his kidney injury. He is undergoing dialysis at present and his condition is stable," said Dr Neerja Bhatla, the AIIMS spokesperson. She said the future course of Suri's treatment will be decided in the next few days.Hospital authorities said over the last three days, a total of 59 patients were brought to AIIMS Trauma Center, of which six needed treatment for fractures.
