<headline>
Fundraiser for Nepal Earthquake Victims; New 'Experience Woodinville' Festival
</headline>

<text>
GREENWOOD — The Olive & Grape is hosting a fundraiser on Sunday for victims of the Nepal earthquake. The evening will involve a buffet dinner from 4 to 8 p.m. and proceeds will go to Doctors Without Borders' Nepal Rescue and Relief efforts. [PhinneyWood]

WOODINVILLE — A new wine and food event called Experience Woodinville will take place June 28 at 5 p.m. The area's wineries, breweries, spirit companies, and restaurants will be present, and there will be a cooking competition to raise money for Little Bit Therapeutic Riding Center. The event takes place at Willows Lodge and tickets are on sale now. [EaterWire]



BELLEVUE — Pagliacci's Crossroads location is now serving pizza by the slice via a new slice bar. They've also extended hours for lunch and late-night dining. [EaterWire]

BELLTOWN — Tavolàta is hosting a wine dinner tomorrow and there are still spots available. Italian wineries Vie di Romans, from Friuli, and Pecchenino, from Dogliani, will both be present to share their wines paired with a multi-course menu. The dinner starts at 7:30 p.m. and reservations can be made by calling (206) 838-8008. [EaterWire]
