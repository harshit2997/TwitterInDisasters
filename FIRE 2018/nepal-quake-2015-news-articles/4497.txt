<headline>
LSU Health Shreveport Grad Student Sets Up Nepal Relief Account
</headline>

<text>
Shreveport, La. - If you watched NBC 6 News at 10pm, you saw the emotional and thoughtful words of Priya Prasai, a graduate student in LSU Health Shreveport's Department of Molecular & Cellular Physiology.

She and her husband lived in Nepal for three years. The country was recently devastated by an earthquake. She is asking for donations for to help the country deal with the devastating aftermath. She wants to make sure all donations go toward reputable organizations, with boots on the ground in Nepal providing help.



If you want to help a "Nepal Earthquake Relief Fund" has been set up through Campus Federal

The Office of Graduate Studies will send half to the American Red Cross and half to the UN's World Food Program in support of Nepal relief efforts. The account number is 10772827.



If you have an account at Campus Federal, donations are accepted electronically.

Campus Federal will deposit donations at their offices and by mail. Please make checks payable to "Nepal Earthquake Relief Fund" and send to:

Campus Federal Credit Union

1501 Kings Highway

Shreveport, LA 71103
