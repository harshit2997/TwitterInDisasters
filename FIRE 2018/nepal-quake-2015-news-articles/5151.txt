With 1,000 Europeans still missing, EU offers urgent support to Nepal
</headline>

<text>
The European Commission has approved today a financial support worth €16.6 million to assist Nepal after the earthquake that hit the country just over a week ago. The assistance will be provided directly to the Government of Nepal and will be available later this week.
