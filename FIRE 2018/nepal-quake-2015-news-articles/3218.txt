<headline>
SGPC sends relief material to Nepal
</headline>

<text>
Shiromani Gurdwara Parbandhak Committee (SGPC) today sent relief material including food items, blankets and utensils to quake-ravaged Nepal.



The relief material consists of refined oil, 300 quintals basmati rice, pulses, spices, gram flour, dry milk powder, bedding mats and blankets and utensils, an SGPC official said.



SGPC Secretary Manjit Singh said a team of doctors comprising of specialists, surgeons and paramedical staff will also be sent to the Himalayan nation for assisting treatment of victims.The Sikh body has also sent a team of cooks to prepare food items for affected people in Nepal, Singh further said.
