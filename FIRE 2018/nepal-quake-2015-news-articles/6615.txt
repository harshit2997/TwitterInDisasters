<headline>
India ready to provide support to Nepal: Rajnath
</headline>

<text>
New Delhi, May 12 (IANS) Home Minister Rajnath Singh on Tuesday said India would provide all the support required to Nepal, which was once again jolted by a major earthquake.

"India is ready to provide all the support which Nepal would require," the minister told reporters.

According to the India Meteorological Department website, a 7.3 magnitude earthquake rocked Nepal again on Tuesday.

Earlier, a 7.9 magnitude earthquake devastated the Himalayan nation on April 25, killing 8,151 people.
