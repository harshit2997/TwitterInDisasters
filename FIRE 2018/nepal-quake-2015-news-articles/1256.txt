<headline>
Chaos and fear reign in wake  of earthquake
</headline>

<text>
Dr. Mabi Singh of Belmont, an associate professor at Tufts University School of Dental Medicine, was visiting family in Kathmandu, Nepal, when the devastating magnitude-7.8 earthquake rocked the nation Saturday. He spoke to the Herald’s Antonio Planas yesterday about the chaos on the streets of the South Asian country’s capital city as survivors and the government grapple with the second day of the disaster.
